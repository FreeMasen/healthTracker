One of the most powerful exercises one can do to take control of their diet is to write down everything they eat. This concept is what drives applications like My Fitness Pal or My Macros +. The main goal of this application is no different than others like it, the thing that sets it apart is the fact that it takes a hands off approach to your data.

Data for this application is stored locally in your browser, that means none of it ever leaves your computer. This approach ensures that you are in control of your data, no one else can intercept, log, track or sell the information. The big draw back here is that your data isn't available on multiple devices. For instance, if you choose to start tracking your information in your home computer, you won't be able to access that information from your cell phone. Currently you can export your information from this site to a file, which could be uploaded into any other browser. The hope is to eventually integrate some cloud storage options like Dropbox or Google Drive.
